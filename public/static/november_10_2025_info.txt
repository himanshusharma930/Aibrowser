November 10, 2025 is significant for several important observances:

1. World Science Day for Peace and Development - Promoted by UNESCO to highlight science's role in promoting peace, sustainability, and global cooperation.

2. World Immunization Day - Established by WHO to raise awareness about the importance of vaccines in preventing diseases and saving lives.

3. World Public Transport Day - Focuses on promoting public transportation as a sustainable and inclusive mobility solution.

4. Janjatiya Gaurav Varsh 2025 - A year-long celebration in India honoring the legacy of tribal freedom fighters.

5. World Orphans Day - Raises awareness about the plight of parentless children.

6. Sporting Achievement - Indian shooter Anish Bhanwala is expected to win a silver medal in the 25m Rapid Fire event at the World Championship.

Historical events that occurred on November 10 in history include:
- 474: Emperor Leo II died after a reign of ten months
- 937: Ten Kingdoms: Li Bian usurped the throne and deposed Emperor Yang Pu
- 1202: Fourth Crusade events
